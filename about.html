Introduction

I am an aspiring software engineer currently enrolled as an undergraduate at Carnegie Mellon University pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer science with a concentration in machine learning. I was born in Pittsburgh, PA and moved to Gainesville, FL in 2008 when I was four years old. I lived in Gainesville until I graduated from Buchholz High School, then went back to Pittsburgh for college. It's funny how some parts of the past always seem to stick with you. I am currently interested in activities such as developing web applications, building deep learning models, and exploring the theory behind everything this world has to offer. My end goal professionally is to be in a flexible software role continuously working on thought-provoking projects that have potential to benefit the lives of future generations.
